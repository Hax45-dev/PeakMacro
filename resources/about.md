# PeakMacro

**Frame-perfect macro recorder & player for Geometry Dash.**

Record your inputs, save them, and replay with pixel-perfect precision.  
Built for practice, showcasing, and fun — by **@Hax25-dev**.

---

## How to use

1. Enter any level
2. Press **P** to open the PeakMacro menu (or use the Pause Menu button)
3. Press **Record** or hit **R** to start recording your run
4. Press **Stop** or **R** again to stop — your macro is auto-saved
5. Press **Play** or **Y** to replay from the beginning

---

## Keybinds

- `P` — Open PeakMacro popup  
- `R` — Toggle recording  
- `Y` — Toggle playback  

---

## Macro files

Macros are saved as `.peak` files in your Geode save directory and can be shared with others.
